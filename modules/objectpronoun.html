Personal pronouns: do the action

Object pronouns: received the action

I YOU HE SHE IT WE THEY
Me You Him Her It Us Them
ME

Pronunciation: [Mi]

Sirve para referirce a mi

For example:

  • Incorrect way: HE CAN GO WITH I
  • Correct way: HE CAN GO WITH ME
YOU

Pronunciation: [iu]

Sirve para referirce a usted(a ti) o a ustedes

For a example: Correct way: I CAN EXPLAIN TO YOU

HIM

Pronunciation: [jim]

Sirve para referirce a él

For example:

  • Incorrect way: I CAN GO WITH HE
  • Correct way: I CAN GO WITH HIM
HER

Pronunciation: [jer]

Sirve para referirce a ella

For example:

  • Incorrect way: I CAN GO WITH SHE
  • Correct way: I CAN GO WITH HER
IT

Pronunciation: [it]

Sirve para referirce a eso

For example:

  • Correct way: You shouldn't buy IT
  • Traduction: no deberías comprarlo
US

Pronunciation: [as]

Sirve para referirce a nosotros

For example:

  • Incorrect way: JUAN CAN'T BE WITH WE
  • Correct way: JUAN CAN'T BE WITH US
THEM

Pronunciation: [dem]

Sirve para referirce a ellos

For example:

  • Incorrect way: ANA SHOULD COME WITH THEY
  • Correct way: ANA SHOULD COME WITH THEM
