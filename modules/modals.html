CAN (poder)

Pronunciation: [ken]

Sirve para referirce al poder hacer algo

For a question:

  • Incorrect way: DO YOU CAN?
  • Correct way:
    • CAN YOU?
    • WHO CAN WORK ON FRIDAY?
    • WHERE CAN I FIND A BANK?
